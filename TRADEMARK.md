## Naming Guidelines for Forks

If you create a fork or derivative work, please choose a distinct name for your
project. Acceptable patterns include:

- "Acme Gateway (based on VeilProxy)"
- "MyProject, powered by VeilProxy"

Unacceptable patterns include:

- "VeilProxy Enterprise Edition"
- "VeilProxy Pro"
- "OpenVeilProxy"

The goal is to avoid confusion about the origin of the software while still
giving credit to the upstream project.
